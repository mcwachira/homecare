What is Pallative Care ?

Pallative care is the providing of treatment and nursing care for anyone living with a serious illness, such as heart failure, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, cancer, dementia, Parkinson's disease, and many others.

In addition to improving quality of life and helping with symptoms, palliative care can help patients understand their choices for medical treatment.

Pallative Care

Our health specialists and nurses are fully trained and experienced to assess your condition and provide to you the most effective treatment to help you live long and comfortable. Our team will help you gain the strength to carry on with daily life and help improve your quality of life.

Our specialists who work together with you, your family and your other doctors so as to provide an extra layer of support when you need it most. In addition to treating your symptoms, the we spends as much time as necessary communicating with both you and your family.

Our service :

  • Provides relief from pain and other distressing symptoms.
  • Affirms life and regards dying as a normal process
  • Integrates the psychological and spiritual aspects of your care.
  • Offers a support system to help you live as actively as possible until death.
  • Offers a support system to help your family cope during the your illness and in their own bereavement.
  • Uses a team approach to address your needs and those of your family, including bereavement and counselling.
  • Will enhance quality of your life, and may also positively influence the course of illness.
